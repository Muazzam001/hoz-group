PRODUCT

HERBAL

Herbalism (“herbology” or “herbal medicine”) is use of plants for medicinal purposes, and the study of such use.Plants have been the basis for medical treatments through much of human history, and such traditional medicine is still widely practiced today.The scope of herbal medicine is sometimes extended to include fungal and bee products, as well as minerals, shells and certain animal parts.

MEDICINE

A pharmaceutical drug (also referred to as a medicine, medication, or medicament) is a drug used to diagnose, cure, treat, or preventdisease.[1][2][3] Drug therapy (pharmacotherapy) is an important part of the medical field and relies on the science of pharmacology for continual advancement and on pharmacy for appropriate management.
